Respect Wildlife and our Animals - Do not attempt to approach, feed, touch, tease or do any act to cause disturbance, annoyance, or injury to any wildlife or our animals. Maintain a safe distance and appreciate them from afar. Any contact with our animals in the Parks for official, entertainment, education, photography, or other authorised purposes shall be provided by or under the supervision of MWG.

Smoke-free Parks Keep our Parks smoke-free.

No Flash Photography or Flashlights Flash photography or flashlights are prohibited to prevent disturbance or annoyance to our animals and other visitors.

No Bicycles, Tricycles, Skateboards, Scooters, Rollerbladers, Skates or Airwheel Luggages Usage of the above items is prohibited in our Parks. Cyclists must park at designated bike parking areas and enjoy our Parks on foot.

Respect Plant Life Please do not pluck, stomp, or damage any plants or climb any trees.

No Releasing of Animals Please do not release or introduce any animals or plants into our Parks as they may harm our animals or eco-system.

No Kite-flying and Playing of Frisbees or Balls The above recreational activities are prohibited to ensure visitor safety and comfort.

No Remote-Controlled Aerial Devices Flying of any model aircrafts (including drones), remote-controlled aerial cameras, or other similar devices in our Parks is not allowed, for visitor safety and protection of our environment.

No Open Fire All forms of open fire, including BBQs and lit candles, are prohibited.

No Fishing or Swimming The above recreational activities are prohibited to ensure visitor safety and protection of our environment.

No balloons in the Ticketed Areas Balloons are not permitted in the Ticketed Areas, in the event of disturbance or annoyance to the animals resulting from a balloon bursting.
